To kick it into gear, we are going to need some help. Specifically, the help of the C++ Standard Library, whose job it is to provide us with many extremely helpful classes which perform common tasks for us, with only a few hits of the keyboard. We've already met iostreams, std::string, and a couple others. But here we're going to meet three of the most helpful components- containers, resource-handlers, and algorithms.
